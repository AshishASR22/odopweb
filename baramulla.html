Baramulla

Baramulla, a picturesque district nestled in Jammu and Kashmir, is often referred to as the 'Gateway of Kashmir'. Beyond its scenic beauty, it is famously known for its luscious apple orchards. As you step into this district, you are greeted with vast stretches of apple trees, their branches laden with vibrant red and green fruits. These apples are not just a treat for the eyes but are renowned for their crisp texture and sweet-tart flavor. Many families in Baramulla have been involved in apple cultivation for generations, with their lives deeply intertwined with the seasonal rhythms of apple farming. From planting saplings to carefully picking the ripe fruits, every step is done with love and dedication. These apples, often termed as the 'Pride of Baramulla', find their way into homes across the country, making Baramulla synonymous with top-quality apples.
