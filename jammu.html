Jammu

Jammu, a region steeped in history and culture, stands proudly at the foothills of the Himalayas. While the picturesque landscapes and historical landmarks are popular attractions, it's the region's milk-based products that capture the hearts and palates of both locals and visitors. With its fertile lands and favorable climate, Jammu's dairy farms produce rich and creamy milk, which serves as the foundation for a variety of delightful products. Be it the velvety yogurts, aromatic paneer (cottage cheese), or the sweetened treats like kheer (rice pudding) and ras malai, every bite transports one to a world of creamy indulgence. These products are not just a testament to the region's culinary heritage but also a celebration of its agrarian roots. For anyone visiting Jammu or exploring its cuisine, the milk-based products offer a delicious and authentic experience, capturing the essence of the land and its traditions.
