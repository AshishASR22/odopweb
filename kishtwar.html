Kishtwar

Kishtwar is renowned for its delectable walnut products. The region's fertile soil and ideal climate provide the perfect conditions for growing high-quality walnuts. Local artisans skillfully craft various walnut-based items, such as walnut kernels, walnut oil, and traditional sweets like walnut fudge. These products are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, offering a blend of taste and health benefits. The walnut kernels are often used as snacks, in desserts, and as toppings for various dishes. The rich, nutty flavor adds a delightful touch to local cuisine. Additionally, walnut oil is a popular choice for cooking and salad dressings due to its unique taste and health advantages. Kishtwar's walnut products are not only a treat for the taste buds but also a source of livelihood for many in the region. They represent the district's rich agricultural heritage and are a cherished part of its culture.
