Srinagar

Srinagar, the heart of the Kashmir Valley, is famous for its delightful bakery products. These mouthwatering treats are a reflection of the rich culinary heritage of the region. The bakeries in Srinagar are known for their aromatic and freshly baked goods. You can find a wide variety of bakery products, from crispy biscuits to soft and fluffy bread. One of the most beloved items is the Kashmiri bread known as "sheermal," a slightly sweet and saffron-infused bread that pairs perfectly with the famous Kashmiri noon chai (salted tea). Moreover, Srinagar is renowned for its traditional cookies, such as shrewsbury biscuits and almond-flavored pastries. These treats are not only delicious but also make for great souvenirs to take back home. Srinagar's bakery products offer a delightful blend of flavors and textures that cater to the sweet tooth of locals and visitors alike.
