Kulgam

Kulgam is known for its delectable spices and pickles that add a burst of flavor to meals. The region's fertile land and favorable climate nurture a variety of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and cardamom. These spices are carefully harvested, dried, and ground to perfection, ensuring top-notch quality. Local artisans in Kulgam also excel in the art of pickling. They preserve seasonal fruits and vegetables in a flavorful concoction of spices, oil, and vinegar. Mangoes, lemons, and mixed vegetables are commonly pickled, creating tangy and spicy delights that can accompany a range of dishes. The pickles and spices from Kulgam are not just about taste; they carry the essence of the region's rich culinary traditions. They make for wonderful souvenirs and additions to your kitchen, promising a delightful journey into the heart of Kashmiri flavors. So, whether you're seeking to spice up your meals or explore new taste sensations, Kulgam's spices and pickles are a must-try.
