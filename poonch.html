Poonch

Poonch is known for its delectable millet-based products. Millets are small, nutritious grains that grow well in the region's climate. The people of Poonch have mastered the art of using millets to create a variety of tasty and wholesome foods. In Poonch, you can find dishes like millet bread, porridge, and even snacks made from these tiny grains. Millets are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making them a healthy choice for those who enjoy local, traditional cuisine. The millet-based products in Poonch are not just about food; they represent a rich cultural heritage and a connection to the land. Many families in Poonch have passed down their millet-based recipes for generations, adding a touch of tradition to every meal.
