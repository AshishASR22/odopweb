Doda

Doda, a picturesque district in the beautiful region of Jammu and Kashmir, is well-known for its production of delicious olive products. Olive cultivation in Doda is an age-old tradition that has been passed down through generations. The local farmers here take great care in growing and harvesting olives, ensuring they are of the finest quality. These olives are then processed to create a variety of products that are not only flavorful but also healthy. In Doda, you can find a range of olive products such as olive oil, pickled olives, and olive-based condiments. Olive oil, in particular, is celebrated for its numerous health benefits and its versatility in cooking. The olives grown in Doda benefit from the region's unique climate and soil conditions, which give them a distinctive and delightful flavor. Whether you use Doda's olive oil for cooking or enjoy the tangy pickled olives as a snack, you're sure to savor the natural goodness and taste of this remarkable district's olive products
