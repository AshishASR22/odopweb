Kupwara

Kupwara, a scenic district nestled in the northern region of Jammu and Kashmir, is not just known for its picturesque landscapes but also for its abundant walnut groves. These walnut trees, with their deep roots in Kupwara's fertile soil, yield some of the finest walnuts in the region. The district takes immense pride in its walnut products, which range from the raw nut to finely crafted walnut wood items. The locals have mastered the art of processing these nuts, ensuring that the walnuts retain their rich taste and nutritional value. The crunchy texture and earthy flavor of Kupwara's walnuts are unparalleled, making them sought-after by both locals and tourists. Moreover, the walnut wood, known for its durability and fine grain, is utilized by local artisans to create intricate handicrafts, symbolizing the district's rich heritage and craftsmanship. In Kupwara, the walnut is not just a nut; it's a testament to the district's bond with nature and tradition.
