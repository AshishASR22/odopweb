Kathua

Kathua is not just a district of scenic beauty but also a hub for aromatic spices. Its fertile lands have made it an ideal ground for growing a variety of spices that form the backbone of many Indian cuisines. From the fiery red chillies to the fragrant turmeric and coriander, Kathua's spice palette is rich and diverse. Local farmers, with generations of expertise, cultivate these spices with utmost care, ensuring that they retain their natural aroma and flavor. The spices from Kathua are not just limited to the local market. They travel across borders, finding their way into kitchens worldwide. For anyone visiting Kathua, a stroll through its spice markets is a sensory delight - a burst of colors, fragrances, and the very essence of Indian culinary traditions. It's a place where tradition meets taste, offering a spice experience like no other.
