Rajouri

Rajouri is known for its delightful milk-based products. Here, the dairy industry thrives, and the local people have mastered the art of producing a wide range of delicious items using milk as their primary ingredient. The lush green pastures of Rajouri provide an ideal environment for cattle to graze, resulting in high-quality milk. This milk is skillfully transformed into various products like creamy yogurt, flavorful cheese, and rich ghee. People in Rajouri take immense pride in their dairy traditions, which have been passed down through generations. One of the most beloved treats from this region is the traditional Rajouri cheese, renowned for its unique taste and texture. Whether enjoyed on its own or incorporated into local recipes, Rajouri's milk-based products are cherished by locals and visitors alike. So, when in Rajouri, don't miss the chance to savor the delightful dairy delights that this charming district has to offer
